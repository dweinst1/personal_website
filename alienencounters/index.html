Alien Encounters is a game of galactic negotiations and social deduction for 2 players.

You are a diplomat representing a species at the Negotiating Table. Both players draw a card that determines their intent - either hostile or peaceful - but neither player knows what their opponent drew. To win, players must gain the appropriate resources, determine their counterpart's intentions, and act accordingly.

Can you trust the player across the Negotiating Table to create a great alliance, or are they deceiving you?
