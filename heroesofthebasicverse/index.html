In this team-based hero brawler, you must coordinate with allies in order to dominate your enemies. Work as a team to slay enemy Heroes and bring their Fortress down!

Each player chooses a unique Hero that has their own role and set of Abilities. Players can react to actions out of turn and are never eliminated from play.

Play smart and become the dominant team in the Basicverse!
